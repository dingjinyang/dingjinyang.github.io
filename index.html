Biography

I am Jinyang Ding (Chinese: 丁锦扬), a third-year master’s student in School of Cyber Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Prof. Weiming Zhang. Prior to that, I got my B.Eng. degree in Information Security from China University of Mining and Technology (CUMT) in 2020.

I am interested in the intersection of security, privacy, and AI. In these two years, my research mainly focuses on provably secure steganography.

I am looking for a PhD position. I’ll be thrilled if you’re interested in my profile. Feel free to contact me by email!
